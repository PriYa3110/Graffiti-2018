About Graffiti

Graffiti is the annual techno managerial fest held at Symbiosis Centre for Information Technology. The two-day event invites students from various technical and business institutions and corporates across India to participate in the wide arena of competitions. It is a seamless and perfect blend of business and technology. The incredible acclaim from the participating students of renowned B-Schools is a testimony to the grandeur and reach of the event. Accentuating the success, we have been exceptionally recognized by prominent corporate houses. The event lives up to its promise of captivating discussions and a rejuvenating experience to all its participants.
